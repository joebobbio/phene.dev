PHENE.DEV PRIVACY POLICY

Thank you for taking an interest in your digital privacy!

Big long legal documents are boring, so I'm going to make this as simple as possible.

Here's what's automatically collected when you visit this website:

  • Your IP address
  • Information about your device, such as
    • Your screen size and resolution
    • Your operating system (OS)
    • What browser you are using
    • Your device form factor (laptop, tablet, mobile phone, etc)
  • What pages you request

Sometimes there may be links to third-party websites on my website. Third-party websites are governed by their own Privacy Policies, and I encourage you to read them.

Sometimes you may provide me additional private information (email address, name, etc) when contacting me or requesting to open a Webmail account. I *never* sell or share your data.

This website uses Google Analytics to help me better understand site traffic and engagement. Should you wish to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking, you may do so by installing this extension or however you normally block this sort of thing.

Thank you for taking the time to read this. If you have any feedback, please email me.
